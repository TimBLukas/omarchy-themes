## Theme Philosophy

Misty Rainforest combines:

- **Ancient forest depths** - Deep green-gray backgrounds like forest shadows
- **Ethereal fog** - Heavy transparency and blur for dreamy atmosphere
- **Moss-covered serenity** - Soft muted green as the signature accent
- **Mountain streams** - Turquoise water tones for vibrancy
- **Cool mist** - Blue-gray fog tones throughout

Perfect for developers who want a peaceful, focused workspace wrapped in the calm of a foggy rainforest sanctuary.
